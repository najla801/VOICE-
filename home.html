About Us

Voice of Hope is an online educational initiative that provides schooling to Afghan girls who are currently deprived of access to formal education. We offer free classes in science, math, languages, and personal development—all taught by volunteer educators.

Our Programs

Online Classes

Daily virtual classes on Google Meet and WhatsApp for grades 7 to 12.

Volunteer Teachers

Skilled teachers from around the world volunteer their time to support Afghan learners.

Workshops

Monthly seminars on mental health, leadership, and digital skills.
